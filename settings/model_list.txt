- Here is a list of models that might be able to run on a less powerful system.
- They are taken from this list: https://ollama.com/library
- Search for new models here: https://ollama.com/search
- 1b, 3b, 3.5b, 8b etc stands for x Billion parameters
	- Higher numbers mean the model will be smarter and give better responses,
		while also running slower and requiring a stronger PC.
